Summary Latitude Vs. Wind Speed

Latitude appears to have no effect on the wind speed in each city. Although the more extreme latitdues appear to have higher wind speeds in this sample, they are most likely outliers due to a lack of a clear correlation amongst the data points. The appears to be an almost even distribution of datapoints with wind speeds around 5 miles per hour across all latitudes.
